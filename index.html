FAQ's

CompSoc runs a variety of events throughout the year, ranging from talks by academics, technical workshops, and a yearly hackathon. We also host plenty of social events, including hikes, barbecues, board game nights, and even a yearly Informatics Ball with a ceilidh.
You can look at the full events calendar here, on our events page. Also, be sure to join our discord server to stay up to date on new events and any changes to event locations or times.
